About Me

I moved to Los Angeles ~3.5 years and it has been quite the journey. Originally from the East Coast, I graduated from NYU with a BSME. I worked for General Dynamics Electric Boat as a Ship Systems Engineer II in the Habitibility & Life Support Systems Group of the VIRGINIA Class Program. Upon moving to LA, I transitioned from engineering and into Project Management.

Since coming to California, I have worked on several contruction projects; USC The Village Project and LAX Terminal Redevelopment Project (T7 & T8). Today, I work at Lendlease Construction (US) as an Assistant Project Manager on the Oceanwide Plaza Project.

I have decided to commit to the Coding Bootcamp because I want to make a change in my life. I have always had an interest in coding but need a structured course to commit to. Outside of work I enjoy rock climbing, skateboarding, hiking & backpacking.
